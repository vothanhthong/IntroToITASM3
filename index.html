Project Description

Overview

Topic
The website would include a task bar with a large screen that presents the clothes design. The task bar would be placed on top of the website with different types of clothes such as pants, t-shirts, shirts, sweaters, ... The website would include different types of clothes (or at least the most popular clothes pieces nowadays) with the customization including colors, shapes, small pieces of accessories, ... As the website’s purpose is to offer users chances to customize their own clothes according to their hobbies, it would also require a design department in order to create a successful website. This appearance is very important with this kind of website.
As the users access the website, the first step is to choose what type of clothes they want to customize. As they click on the kind of clothes, the most basic image of that clothes would be presented on the customizing screen. At the bottom of the screen, at the same time, there will be a customizing bar displayed which might include colors for different parts of the clothes, shape, accessories and some simple images that users can choose to put on the clothes. The user can also insert the pictures from their devices to put on the clothes as their want according to their own style.

Motivation
The reason we think this website is interesting is that until now, there are still very few websites that enable users to customize their own clothes. This website give user opportunity to put their own style into the clothes. Everybody likes to own at least one piece of clothes that is unique and show their characteristics, their styles which no one can buy or find.

Landscape
There are some typical Outfit customize websites that already existed such as customink.com, contrado.co.uk, spreadshirt.com, apliiq.com and a typical website that manufacturing in Vietnam is t-shirt-printing-vietnam.com.
These websites share a common thing is that they provide user to customize based on an existed product for only change the figure on the item. What make our website stands out is give user opportunity to have adjustment on the very detail parts of the outfits, which make the outfits unique.

Detailed Description

Aims
The first intention of the team was only to working on the most feasible idea based on the team skills and abilities. The more we worked on the project, the more enthusiasm we put on the project. As the website provide user the easiest way to shopping clothes, with the unique and have not been developed before, we think the outcome website can probably change the way people shopping if it is developed intensively.
The aftermath of the Covid-19 outbreak has forced everyone to go online with their enterprises. People no longer like to purchase at a market or mall; instead, they prefer to search or browse online for new arrivals and accessories. This also allows them to save time and effort. A task bar with a large screen displaying the clothing design would be included on the website. The task bar would be placed on top of the website and would include various clothing items such as slacks, t-shirts, shirts, sweaters, and so on. When customers initially visit the website, they must first decide what style of clothing they want to create. When users select a type of clothing, the most basic image of that clothing is displayed on the customization screen. A customizing bar will be displayed at the bottom of the screen, which may include colors for different portions of the clothes, shape, accessories, and some simple graphics that users can pick to put on the outfits. The user has the option to insert figure and images from their mobile devices as well. Users can view their finished goods and decide whether or not to proceed to the payment procedure after finishing customizing their apparel. Users can always go back to the previous steps and start customizing their products again if they aren't content with the things they have created. Customers can complete the payment stage and choose whether to pick up their purchases in the store or have them mailed if they are satisfied with the product.
The process of customizing, checking, paying and shipping are provided to the users and how detail the users can customize as discussed above can really help us achieve our goal.
